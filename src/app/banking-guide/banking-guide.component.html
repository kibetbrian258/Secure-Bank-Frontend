Getting Started

Navigating Your Dashboard

Your dashboard is the central hub for managing your finances. Here's what you'll find:

  • Account Overview: View balances and recent transactions for all your accounts.
  • Quick Actions: Easily access common tasks like deposits, transfers, and bill payments.
  • Financial Summary: Get a snapshot of your spending and savings trends.
  • Notifications: Stay updated with important alerts about your accounts.

Customize your dashboard by clicking the "Personalize" option in the top right corner to show the information most relevant to you.

Account Management

Understanding Your Account Types

Savings Account

Earn interest on your deposits while maintaining easy access to your funds.

  • Interest rate: 2.5% p.a.
  • Minimum balance: Ksh 200
  • No monthly fee

Checking Account

Your everyday spending account for regular transactions and bill payments.

  • No interest
  • No minimum balance
  • Monthly fee: Ksh 5

Managing Account Settings

You can customize various settings for your accounts:

  • Account Nicknames: Rename your accounts for easier identification.
  • Statement Preferences: Choose between electronic or paper statements.
  • Transaction Alerts: Set up notifications for specific transaction types or amounts.
  • Authorized Users: Add or remove people who can access your account.

Transactions

Making Deposits

There are several ways to add funds to your account:

  1. Direct Deposit: Set up automatic deposits from your employer or other income sources.
  2. Mobile Deposit: Use the app to deposit checks by taking photos.
  3. Branch Deposit: Visit any of our branches during operating hours.
  4. ATM Deposit: Use our network of ATMs for cash or check deposits.

Transferring Funds

Move money between your accounts or send to others:

  • Internal Transfers: Move money between your own accounts instantly.
  • External Transfers: Send money to accounts at other banks (1-2 business days).
  • Recurring Transfers: Schedule automatic transfers on a regular basis.
  • Wire Transfers: Send larger amounts domestically or internationally for a fee.

Set up recurring transfers to your savings account to automate your saving habits.

Transaction History

Keep track of your financial activity:

  • View up to 24 months of transaction history online.
  • Filter transactions by date, amount, or type.
  • Search for specific transactions using keywords.
  • Download statements in PDF or CSV format.
  • Categorize transactions for better budget tracking.
